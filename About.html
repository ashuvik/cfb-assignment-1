Crime Free Bharat armed with five-pronged strategies:—
1. Certainty of registration of FIR
2. Certainty of Detection with 21 technologies at the service of the Investigation Officer.
3. Certainty of Conviction: with plenty of digital and non-digital infallible evidence armed with a certificate of integrity, certificate of admissibility under section 65B Evidence Act, and certificate of a chain of custody.
4. Certainty of Prevention through Dynamic and Proactive deployment of police and community volunteers
5. Certainty of Rehabilitation of criminals

1. Job and services Rich Bharat with trained and specialized manpower
2. Green, Herbal, Medicine Free, and Healthy Bharat
3. Milk Rich Bharat with a cow as mother cow and Vedic culture
4. Machine, Equipment and tool Rich Bharat by pooling resources from owners clubs
5. Creative, Innovative and dynamic Bharat
6. Aatm Nirbhar and Samarth Bharat
7. Ground water Rich Bharat
